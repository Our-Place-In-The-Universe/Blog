Dawn of the planet

Precambrian period

Approximately 4 billion years ago, Earth was a molten crucible, revolving around a young host star, at that time the solid core was been formed from liquid silicon and iron.

Around 3 billion years ago, came ancient microbes tiny, resilient, and tenacious They began their quiet revolution and thrived in primordial oceans, harnessing sunlight or volcanic heat. These pioneers, devoid of limbs or eyes, shaped the blueprint of life.

Cambrian period

Then came the cambrian period around 600 million years ago, microbial soloists had evolved into Multicellular organisms—some with hard shells, others soft, This period is often refered to as the "Cambrian Explosion", The sea was filled with all variety of creatures ranging from anthropods to invertibrates.
